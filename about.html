About

Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College (ISPSC) is a multi-campus institution dedicated to providing professional and technical education at both undergraduate and graduate levels. It offers programs in diverse fields such as economics, agriculture, fisheries, trade, home industries, engineering, education, forestry, management, finance, accounting, business administration, public administration, and more. The institution also focuses on promoting scientific and technological research. While the main campus is in Santa Maria, Ilocos Sur, the Candon City campus, located in Darapidap, stands out as the mainstream campus for the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program.

ISPSC BSIT CANDON CAMPUS

The BS in Information Technology program includes the study of the utilization of both hardware and software technologies involving planning, installing, customizing, operating, managing and administering and maintaining information technology infrastructure that provides computing solutions to address the needs of an organization. The program prepares graduates to address various user needs involving the selection, development, application, integration and management of computing technologies within an organization.
